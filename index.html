Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Fuel Prices CY (“the App”) handles user information.

The App is designed to provide users with up-to-date fuel prices across Cyprus and help them find nearby fuel stations.

1. Data We Collect

a. Location Data

The App may access your device’s location to display nearby fuel stations and relevant fuel prices. Location data is used only within the App and is not linked to your identity.

b. Diagnostics Data

The App may collect anonymous diagnostics data, such as crash reports and performance information. This data helps improve the stability and performance of the App. Diagnostics data is not linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide location-based fuel price information
  • Improve app performance and reliability

3. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. If third-party services are used, they process data in an aggregated and non-identifiable manner.

4. Data Storage

The App does not store personal user data on external servers. Any data processed is used temporarily and only as needed for app functionality.

5. Your Privacy Choices

You can disable location access at any time through your device settings. If location access is disabled, some features of the App may not function properly.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
